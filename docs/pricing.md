# Pricing

> **Note:** The following pricing structure and features are subject to change and are provided for discussion purposes only.

## Open Questions
- Should subscription plans be based on Sync features (e.g., use of our cloud database/file storage vs. local only)?

---

## Free Tier
- **File Limit:** Limited number of files (e.g., 1 setting, X locations, Y characters, Z monsters, etc., or a total file limit)
- **Templates:** Limited selection
- **Integrations:** Limited options
- **Library Access:** Limited copy/paste from Library
- **AI Credits:** May include a small number of AI credits

---

## Base Plan – $8/month
- **Files:** Unlimited
- **Library:** Unlimited sharing/using of Library assets
- **Templates:** Access to all templates
- **Integrations:** Access to all integrations
- **AI Credits:** Normal allocation per month

---

## Pro Plan – $20/month
- **AI Credits:** Increased monthly amount (best deal)
- **On-Demand AI:** Access included
- **API:** Transparent, pay-as-you-go pricing for additional AI credits
